# Design Specification: Publications Archive

**Purpose:**
A structured, academic-grade record of professional research, patents, and workplace contributions.

## Layout & Components

### Page Header

- **Headline:** "Publications" in bold Space Grotesk, half-white/half-pink styling.

### Section Headers

- Categorized into "Journal Articles", "Conference Proceedings", and "Patents & Intellectual Property".

### Publication Row

- **Metadata:** Includes Year, DOI/PDF links, and Company Attribution.
- **Attribution Marker:** Clear labels for workplace research.
- **CTA Buttons:** Sleek icons for [DOI] or [PDF] downloads.

### Styling

- Tabular list layout for high information density.
- Neon purple hover highlight consistent with the Projects page.
