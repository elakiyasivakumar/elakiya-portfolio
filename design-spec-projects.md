# Design Specification: Projects List

**Purpose:**
An interactive showcase of technical case studies and creative projects, emphasizing a sleek, list-based browsing experience.

## Layout & Components

### Page Header

- **Headline:** "Projects" in bold Space Grotesk, half-white/half-pink styling.

### Project List Item

- **Structure:** Horizontal layout with Image (Left) and Content (Right).
- **Image:** High-contrast placeholder with subtle inner glow.
- **Content:** Title, brief description, and category tags.
- **Tags:** [Biomedical Engineering] or [AI] - styled as neon pill outlines.

### Interaction Logic

- **Hover State:** The entire row container transitions its border to Neon Purple and adds a soft glow effect.
- **Z-Index:** On hover, the row elevates slightly to provide depth.
