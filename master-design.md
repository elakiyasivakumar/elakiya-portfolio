# Design System Document

This document outlines the core design principles and token values for our digital products.

## Theme Overview

Our theme is designed with a **dark** aesthetic.

## Color Palette

- **Primary Color:** `#B829EA` - This vibrant magenta serves as our primary brand color, ideal for prominent interactive elements.
- **Secondary Color:** `#FFB5E8` - A lighter, complementary pink, used for supporting UI elements and secondary actions.
- **Tertiary Color:** `#8B00FF` - A deep purple accent, providing highlights and decorative touches.
- **Neutral Color:** `#0B0A10` - A very dark, almost black, neutral for backgrounds and surfaces, ensuring strong contrast.

## Typography

- **Headlines:** `spaceGrotesk` - Provides a modern and distinct voice for all headings.
- **Body Text:** `beVietnamPro` - Chosen for its legibility and friendly appearance in longer content.
- **Labels:** `spaceGrotesk` - Consistent with headlines, ensuring clear and concise labeling.

## Shape and Spacing

- **Roundedness:** `2` (Moderate) - Corners have a gentle curve, balancing approachability with structure.
- **Spacing:** `2` (Normal) - A standard amount of whitespace, providing good visual separation without feeling sparse or cluttered.
